About

I'm a graduate researcher interested in theoretical reinforcement learning, RL on large language models, multi-agent systems and applied nlp. My work currently focuses on bandit algorithms and agentic AI.

I'm presently a student researcher in the CFILT lab at IIT Bombay, where I work on above mentioned areas. I completed my B.Tech. at IIT Kanpur in 2025.

Research

Theoretical Reinforcement Learning

I study computational problems in game-theoretic settings, focusing on equilibrium computation and mechanism design. My work bridges theoretical computer science and economics to develop efficient algorithms for strategic interactions.

Multi Agent Learning

I investigate how autonomous agents can learn to coordinate and cooperate in dynamic environments. This includes reinforcement learning, online learning, and adaptive mechanisms for multi-agent systems.

Applied NLP

I work on ensuring AI systems behave in accordance with human values and intentions, particularly in multi-agent scenarios where strategic behavior can lead to misalignment.
